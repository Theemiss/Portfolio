Overview

I co-founded DarDev Solutions in 2023 and serve as CTO. The company builds SaaS products and delivers client engagements from Tunis. This page covers the internal platform and product architecture — separate from the DarDev marketing site at dardev.net.

The challenge

A small engineering organization needs enterprise-grade platform discipline — unified identity, developer environments, observability, and delivery standards — without enterprise headcount. In 2026 we shipped two new products while maintaining client delivery and internal operations.

Architecture

DarDev Workspace is the company identity and operations hub. DarDevLab provides cloud development environments. DevOps and monitoring wrap production workloads. A CRM and mail stack handles licensing, ops workflows, and product manifests.

Key decisions

Platform before features
